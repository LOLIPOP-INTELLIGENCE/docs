## Basic Research Workflows

### Competitive Analysis

```text
"Research our top 3 competitors:
- Visit their websites and document their features
- Check their pricing pages
- Find recent news about them
- Look for customer reviews
- Create a comparison table in Notion"
```

### Market Research

```text
"Research the AI automation market:
- Find market size and growth projections
- Identify top 10 players
- List emerging trends
- Gather recent funding news
- Summarize in a Google Doc with sources"
```

### People Research

```text
"Before my meeting tomorrow, research [Name]:
- Check their LinkedIn profile
- Find recent posts or articles
- Look for mutual connections
- Note their company's recent news
- Create a brief in my meeting notes"
```

## Multi-Source Research

### Academic Research

```text
"Research [topic] for my paper:
- Find peer-reviewed articles
- Summarize key findings
- Identify research gaps
- Create bibliography
- Highlight conflicting views"
```

### Product Research

```text
"Before we build [feature]:
- Research similar features in other products
- Find user complaints about current solutions
- Analyze implementation approaches
- Estimate market demand
- Create PRD draft with findings"
```

### Legal & Compliance

```text
"Research regulations for [industry] in [location]:
- Find current requirements
- Track recent changes
- Identify compliance needs
- Note penalties for violations
- Create compliance checklist"
```

## Pro Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Set research boundaries">
    "Define scope upfront: 'Focus on B2B SaaS companies with 50-500 employees in the US'"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Specify output format">
    "Tell Kairos how you want results: 'Create an executive summary with bullet points, then detailed findings'"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Request confidence levels">
    "Ask for certainty indicators: 'Note whether each finding is confirmed by multiple sources'"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Enable continuous monitoring">
    "Set up ongoing research: 'Check for new information on this topic every week and alert me to changes'"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Complex Research Examples

### Patent Landscape

```text
"Research patents in [technology area]:
1. Find relevant patents filed in last 5 years
2. Identify key players and inventors
3. Map technology trends
4. Highlight potential conflicts with our work
5. Suggest innovation opportunities"
```

### Customer Journey Mapping

```text
"Research how customers buy [product type]:
1. Find online discussions and reviews
2. Map typical research process
3. Identify decision factors
4. Note pain points and objections
5. Create buyer journey visualization"
```

<Note>
  **Quality tip**: Always ask Kairos to cite sources and flag any uncertain information. Better to know the limits of available data than to work with assumptions.
</Note>
